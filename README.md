# A.S.T.R.A. — Advanced Scientific Technology for Resource Acceleration

ASTRA is a Minecraft mod focused on realistic technology, physics, and thermo-fluid dynamics, aiming to simulate complex industrial processes at a deeper level.
The mod lets you experience systems inspired by real-world engineering, such as working in an advanced oil refinery, where temperature, pressure, fluid behavior, and chemical interactions all play a critical role.

ASTRA pushes Minecraft technology to a new level of complexity, emphasizing:
realistic industrial processing
environmental simulation
multi-stage resource refinement
physics-driven systems rather than simplified mechanics.

The goal is to create a highly immersive and systematic technological framework where efficiency, environment, and engineering design all matter.
